CADDICT is a design hackathon conducted by ASME-VIT. It will test the application of engineering concepts of students to solve everyday problems based on sustainable development goals.

Rules of Participantion

  1. Minimum of one person and maximum of three people per team.

  2. The team found using unfair means will be disqualified and barred from participating in any other events conducted by ASME-VIT.

  3. Teams can re-participate in the remaining events of the Hack-A-Thon if they wish to try for the final event. Winning teams will not be allowed to participate in the rest of the events; they will be moving on directly to the final event.
