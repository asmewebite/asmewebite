Recruitments 2022

INSTRUCTIONS :

  1. Read all the instructions carefully before filling the form.
  2. You can select three choices out of six departments. Familiarise yourself with each department by reading about them in the home page of our website for your reference.
  3. Only first year students of the Vellore campus are eligible to apply.
  4. All the students registering should register in VTOP as well under “American Society of Mechanical Engineers”.
  5. Once registered, an official e-mail will be sent to you for further rounds of recruitments. Make sure to check your mail for further instructions.

DESCRIPTIONS :

  • Events: The Events Department is responsible for managing the whole process from the planning stage, right through to running the event and carrying out the post-event evaluation. It responsible for successfully organizing our international symposium as well as all the events that take place.

  • Outreach: The Outreach Department works to boost the public relations and outreach of the chapter. Its responsibility is to decide events and outreach content and reach out to national as well as international speakers for events.

  • Publicity: The Publicity Department takes care of promotions and publicity across all social media platforms. It is responsible for publicity and reach for all upcoming events.

  • Design & Media: The Design department is where the creative ideas are transformed into catchy posters for promotion. It is responsible for creating posters and reels to be shared on social media platforms.

  • Editorial: The Editorial department is where ideas are converted into thoughts and words and a perfect place to be if you have a knack for words. It is responsible for the captions of Instagram posts and the monthly blogs.

  • Halls and DPNR: The Halls and DPNR Department takes care of all the documentation and ensures that the reports are generated on time. Its is responsible for procurement of permissions and maintaining attendance records for numerous events.
