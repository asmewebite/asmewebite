MECH-A-THON 2021

MECH-A-THON is one of most pre-eminent Hack-a-thon for Mechanical engineers which was scheduled this year from 30th of April 2021 to 2nd of May 2021.

An introductory session was hosted on Day 1 of MECH-A-THON in the benevolent presence of esteemed faculty in charges, judges panel and participants. The participants were provided with an industrial problem statement provided by our industry partner Chola Design Solutions. They have to come up with an efficient and effective solution for the problem based on which they were judged through different rounds.

1st review session was conducted on Day 1 consisting of four panels.
