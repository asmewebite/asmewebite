Futuristic view of Sustainable Aircraft Design

ASME-VIT is back with another exciting webinar on the topic "Futuristic view of Sustainable Aircraft Design‚Äù

The staggering amount of carbon dioxide produced by aircrafts has been highlight of debates for the last few years and organizations have been looking solutions for it. Thus, the next-generation air transportation should be a key to influence the environment, safety, and the economy. Several programs are trying to create emerging innovation towards sustainability, system integrity, and alternative fuels to guarantee a reduce in its environmental effect as greenhouse gas. Nowadays, the aerospace industry is looking forward to aviation sustainable development around the globe. Join this session to know more about this topic.
